## 🧭 About Me

I'm a **Cloud Engineer & DevOps Engineer** focused on building reliable, scalable, and secure cloud-native infrastructure. I design production AWS environments, automate everything that moves with Terraform & CI/CD, and obsess over observability, cost optimization, and developer experience.

I'm also a **Co-Founder at [SolidPoint.ai](https://solidpoint.ai/)** — an AI-powered content summarization platform for YouTube, Reddit, and web articles — where I own cloud operations, containerized deployments, CI/CD, and infrastructure reliability end-to-end.

I'm a self-driven learner who reads docs instead of tutorials, prefers open-source tools, and documents every system I touch. Currently finishing my BSc in Computer Science at Arish University while shipping production work.
